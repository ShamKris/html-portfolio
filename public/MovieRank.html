All-time top Malayalam Movies

Hridayam

Based on real college life event, this film brings joy, despair in the eyes of Arun Neelakandan. How he turns his college life around for a better future.

Pappan

One of the best performance Surech Gopi as a cop and his son being a perfect supporting actor and its their first father son duo following the steps of Jayaram, Mohanlal.

Ravanaprabhu

Another Mohanlal masterpiece, a sequel to his other film devasuram. He does a double role as father and son, the story of this film thrills the aspect of one person fighting for his family heirtage in the hands of his family's enemy.
